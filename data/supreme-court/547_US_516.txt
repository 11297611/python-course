PER CURIAM. 
The writ of certiorari limited to Question 1 presented by the petition, granted at 546 U.S. 1075, 126 S. Ct. 830, 163 L. Ed. 2d 705 (2005), is dismissed as improvidently granted. Ideal Steel Supply Corp., ante, p. 451, 126 S. Ct. 1991, 164 L. Ed. 2d 720.  
It is so ordered.
