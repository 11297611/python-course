OPINION
The petitions for writs of certiorari are granted, the judgments are vacated and the cases are remanded to the respective United States Courts of Appeals for further consideration in light of Breen v. Selective Service Board, 396 U.S. 460.
MR. JUSTICE HARLAN would reverse the judgments in these cases and remand them on the basis of his concurring opinion in Breen v. Selective Service Board, 396 U.S., at 468, and the Court's opinion in Gutknecht v. United States, 396 U.S. 295.
